Personal Background

Early Life and Background

I am Kim Princes R. Villanueva, 21 years old, and I grew up in Purok 5, Brgy. Tibig, Lipa City, Batangas. Raised in a simple but supportive family, education has always been a priority in our home. From childhood, I developed a passion for art and creativity, which led me to actively participate in departmental activities throughout my academic life.

Why I Chose Criminology

My dream has always been to serve my community. During Senior High School, I worked as an Assistant Teacher from 2022 to 2023. That experience taught me discipline, communication, and genuine care for others. It deepened my desire to contribute more broadly to society, which led me to pursue Criminology.
